Rules

  • There is a solution to each problem in plain English (Commonwealth spelling), Roman letters, no punctuation and no case.

  • I won’t confirm the correctness of the answers. You will know if you have it right because it will be a proper English sentence.

  • You may not use a computer program unless you write it yourself (and send me the source / method with your answer.)

  • You only may share the work if you agree to share the bounty equally.

  • If no one gets all three, half the bounty will go to whoever’s first with two of any of the answers. If no one gets at least two answers, there will be no bounty awarded to anyone. You may as well send the answers to me as you come up with them.

  • These rules might change subtly. The main thrust of them is consistent and the ciphertext won’t change. See the changes section.

  • I’m the judge.

Eligibility

To be eligible to enter, you must have received the challenge directly from me, in writing. That is, you can’t have simply found this document and expect to receive the bounty. I must have sent the an email directly to you or to a group to which you belong. Again, I’m the judge. If you haven’t received the challenge and you think you should have, please let me know.

Submission

Submission must be done by email, no exceptions. My email address is huw dot rowlands at ieee dot org.

Expiry

Answers must be in by 11:59 on 24 December 2011, Australian Daylight Savings Time. That is, you have until Christmas Day to submit answers.

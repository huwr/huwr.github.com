Chocolate Sauce Pudding

  • 60 g butter
  • 1/2 cup castor sugar
  • dash vanilla essence
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa
  • 1 cup self-raising flour
  • 1 cup milk

Topping: combine in a saucepan and heat until boiling the following: 1/2 cup sugar, 1 tablespoon cocoa, 1 1/2 cup boiling water

Cream butter, sugar and essence. Add egg, beat until fluffy. Fold in half the sifted cocoa and self-raising flour then cut in half the mix. Repeat with remaining flour, cocoa and milk mix. Pour mixture into a 1.5 litre capacity oven proof baking dish (not a spring-form pan). Carefully pour the topping over the cake batter. Bake in a hot over for 30-40 minutes (the cake batter will rise to the surface and the topping will form a sauce underneath). Serve hot

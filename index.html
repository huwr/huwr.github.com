Huw's LaTeX template

Gimme it!

I have been using LaTeX for a while. I really like it. A problem with products like Microsoft Word is that it fails to separate the two concerns of writing and typesetting. That is, it muddles up the writing of a thoughtful essay with the document's typesetting and layout. In LaTeX, however, an author writes a document in plain text, free of the distraction of typesetting and layout. When finished, or ready to draft, the author uses LaTeX to typeset. It's a markup language.

I've been doing it for ages, and I take great joy in fiddling with the corners of it. Loads of people find that aspect tedious. I totally understand that. Hopefully my template will prove useful to you.

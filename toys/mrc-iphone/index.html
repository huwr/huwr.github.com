What?

We are the Murrumbidgee Rover Crew. We're a Rover Scout crew in the Canberra, ACT. Rovers are guys and girls between 18 and 26 years old and like to have fun and get up to lots of tricks. We have adventures and are all very good friends. We get to go on long trips to odd places, put on big theatrical productions, learn about various skills and all sorts of stuff. Look at our programme to see what we're up to next.

You can come join us, if you like! We meet at the North Kambah Scout Hall, Tuesdays at 7.30 PM. Meetings generally end around 9.30 PM with either a trip to the pub or ice cream - depends.

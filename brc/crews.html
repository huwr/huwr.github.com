Lake Burley Griffin Yarralumla

Located in the central suburb of Yarralumla in Canberra, on the shore of Lake Burley Griffin, LBG Rover Crew is an enthusiastic, fun-loving group of rovers.

  • We like to go sailing, canoeing and kayaking in summer. We have access to some great boats and equipment, as well as the best lake in Canberra!
  • We also love doing craft activities, going to the movies, playing board games, and generally just hanging out while having fun together.
  • In addition, a number of our crew have their rock qualifications which means rock climbing, caving and abseiling are all features of our programme.
  • A number of our members are working towards their BP awards. As result we like to run themed camps, develop new skills, and act in service to the broader community.
  • LBG Rovers participate in a developed, well rounded programme: This includes physical, spiritual, social, and creative activities.

There is a place for everyone at LBG Rover Crew. Come along!

But it is named after a Leisure Centre that has a pool in it, so it is water related some how.

Murrumbidgee Kambah, Tuggeranong

Murrumbidgee Rovers is the largest crew in Canberra.

We participate in a good variety of things. Because of our size, we have a broad set of skills and interests in our crew, including 4WDing, theatre (on stage and techinical) and events. Many of our rovers are heavily involved with the Gang Show, and Murrumbidgee has produced many famous bases for the yearly Dragon Skin event in New South Wales.

The programme is diverse and includes much encouragement for rovers to do their Baden-Powell Scout Award, of which we have many awardees.

We even have a website. Look!

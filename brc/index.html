What is this page?

This is a demo page.

It is only intended to show one idea of how the ACT Rovers site could be reformed. Currently, there are many different 'sites', made at different times by different people and with different intentions. They are out of date. This one makes no attempt at being in date, and nor should it: it's a demo. I've just used Bootstrap from Twitter to bash together something simple... That, and it's hosted on GitHub, which is entirely inappropriate...

If you want the source because you're going to move it to somewhere appropriate and for whatever reason you're using this as a starter, you probably shouldn't be. Go grab the best version of Bootstrap from somewhere else, or use HTML5 Boilerplate, or that thing that merges together Boilerplate and Bootstrap. Use one of those...

But if you want it is of course available: the source code. But I urge you not to put it into production...
